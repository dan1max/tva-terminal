Threat Level Classifications

EXTREME: Variant poses immediate threat to Sacred Timeline integrity. Capable of creating multiple branch timelines or resisting TVA apprehension. Requires authorization from Judge or Time-Keepers for engagement.

HIGH: Variant demonstrates significant temporal manipulation abilities or has caused major nexus events. Standard Minutemen protocols may be insufficient.

MEDIUM: Variant has caused measurable timeline deviation but poses manageable threat. Standard apprehension protocols applicable.

LOW: Minor deviation from Sacred Timeline. Minimal threat to temporal stability. Routine pruning procedures sufficient.

Status Definitions

AT LARGE: Variant location unknown. Active search in progress.

ESCAPED: Variant was in TVA custody but escaped. High priority target.

DETAINED: Variant in TVA custody awaiting trial and processing.

PRUNED: Variant and associated timeline branch have been permanently removed from existence.
